## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Hero messaging emphasizes long-term wealth building
The system SHALL present hero copy that frames wealth building as a marathon, highlighting discipline and consistent tracking of assets and liabilities over time.

#### Scenario: Hero conveys long-term message
- **WHEN** a visitor loads the home page
- **THEN** the hero headline and supporting text state that wealth building is a long-term journey requiring discipline and tracking.

### Requirement: Supporting section reinforces tracking habit
The system SHALL include supporting copy near the hero or chart section that invites users to track assets and liabilities over the long term to reach goals.

#### Scenario: Supporting copy encourages tracking
- **WHEN** the visitor views the section adjacent to the chart/visuals
- **THEN** the copy encourages ongoing tracking of assets and liabilities to achieve goals and underscores steady progress.

### Requirement: CTA aligns with disciplined progress
The system SHALL provide a primary call-to-action that invites users to start tracking their net worth, consistent with the long-term, disciplined message.

#### Scenario: CTA reflects tracking value
- **WHEN** the visitor views the hero
- **THEN** the primary CTA label aligns with starting or continuing tracking (e.g., “Start tracking your net worth”).
