## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Show multi-year asset vs liability trend
The system SHALL display an illustrative chart on the home page that shows assets and liabilities over multiple years (minimum 3 distinct yearly points) to communicate long-term tracking.

#### Scenario: Home page renders asset/liability chart
- **WHEN** a visitor loads the home page
- **THEN** the page renders a visible chart (image or lightweight render) comparing assets and liabilities across multiple years with a legend for both series.

### Requirement: Provide accessible context for the trend chart
The system SHALL provide descriptive alt text and a caption that explain the purpose of the chart and clarify that values are illustrative.

#### Scenario: Chart accessibility
- **WHEN** the chart is present on the home page
- **THEN** it includes alt text describing the multi-year asset vs liability trend and a caption noting it is an illustrative example.

### Requirement: Highlight disciplined, long-term progress near the chart
The system SHALL pair the chart with copy that reinforces wealth building as a long-term, disciplined journey and encourages ongoing tracking.

#### Scenario: Chart accompanies long-term messaging
- **WHEN** the user views the chart section
- **THEN** adjacent copy states that wealth building is a marathon (not a sprint) and invites users to track assets and liabilities over time.
