## Why

The home page currently underplays the idea that wealth building is a long-term, disciplined journey. Visitors need to understand that this product helps them track assets and liabilities over time, not chase quick wins. Reframing the message as a marathon builds trust, sets realistic expectations, and increases conversion for users who value steady progress.

## What Changes

- Reframe hero and supporting copy to emphasize long-term, disciplined wealth building (a marathon, not a sprint).
- Add illustrative graphs showing multi-year movement of assets and liabilities to convey the product’s tracking focus.
- Align visuals and copy to set expectations about steady progress and the value of ongoing tracking.

## Capabilities

### Modified Capabilities
- home-page: Update hero messaging, supporting copy, and imagery to emphasize long-term wealth-building discipline and progress tracking.

## Impact

- Frontend: Update home page layout, hero section, and supporting sections; add chart/graph assets or components.
- Assets: Add static illustrative graphs (images or lightweight chart renders) demonstrating multi-year asset vs. liability trends over several years.
- Content: New copy focused on long-term discipline, habit-building, and tracking benefits.
